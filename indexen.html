Welcome

In the heart of Aarhus lies the cultural street Mejlgade, where Oli Bistro is located. A French and authentic bistro. The food is simple and customized to the wishes of the customers - sometimes inspired by other kitchens.
Our mission is to create a welcoming and welcoming bistro where you can expect a rather informal and cozy atmosphere and where the quality is high and the price is low.

Check out the bistro and feel cozy!
